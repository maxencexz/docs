  <Accordion title="No NSFW Content"  icon="skull">
    Freejourney strictly prohibits the use of NSFW (Not Safe for Work) content in any form. This includes, but is not limited to, sexually explicit imagery, offensive language, violent or graphic material, and any other inappropriate content. Freejourney aims to be a safe and inclusive platform for all users, and maintaining a respectful environment is a top priority. Below is a breakdown of what qualifies as NSFW content, potential violations, and the consequences of breaking this rule.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sexual content">
        Any form of nudity, sexually explicit imagery, or descriptions in text-based prompts. This includes both realistic and stylized depictions (such as anime or cartoons).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Violence and gore">
        Images or text prompts that depict extreme violence, graphic injuries, or any form of brutality.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Hate speech or offensive material">
        Prompts or images that contain derogatory language, hate symbols, or content that promotes discrimination or harm based on race, gender, sexuality, religion, etc.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Illegal activities">
        Content that promotes or encourages illegal activities, including drug use, criminal behavior, or any unlawful acts.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Warning>If the user holds a paid subscription, it will be revoked immediately. As outlined in our Terms of Service, refunds are not provided in cases of violations leading to account suspension or termination.</Warning>
