## Welcome to MaxAI!

MaxAI, originally founded in 2021 under the name PrismAI, was established with a clear mission: to democratize artificial intelligence by making it accessible and affordable for everyone. Our API delivers reliable and intuitive AI tools that empower developers and businesses to innovate, build, and scale without constraint. Since our inception, we have expanded our platform to provide a comprehensive suite of accessible AI solutions, including:

- Image generation from text prompts
- Image editing using natural human language instructions
- High-resolution upscaling
- Background removal and replacement
- Video generation from text or reference imagery
